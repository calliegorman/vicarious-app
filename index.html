More time for traveling.
More peace of mind for family and friends.

Solo travelers, adventurous travelers, and even novice travelers know the struggle of keeping in touch with and ensuring loved ones they are safe during their travels.


At vicarious, we love the family and friends who want to live vicariously through your travel experiences and make sure you are safe.

Vicarious is an app that:


  • 1. Prompts you daily to track fun, brief details about your travels.
  • 2. Helps keep loved ones in the know while you’re away.
  • 3. Provides a unique view of your trip with our trip overview feature.

Track offline

Vicarious can track your location and steps while off the grid so you can share later once connected.

Save battery

The app doesn’t need much battery to run, so rest assured that you’ll never run out of power.

Involve loved ones

Family and friends can receive notifications when you post an entry and can comment on and like them.

Never forget

Daily push notifications and reminders will ensure you never forget to complete a entry.
